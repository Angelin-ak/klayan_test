Maintaining Your Jewellery Machinery for Longevity

High-performance machinery is an investment. To ensure that your workshop continues to operate at peak efficiency, regular maintenance is not just recommended—it's essential.

Daily & Weekly Checklist

Small habits can prevent major breakdowns. Here are some key maintenance tasks every jewellery manufacturer should follow:

  • ✔ Clean Surfaces: Remove metal dust and debris from moving parts daily.
  • ✔ Lubrication: Ensure all gears and bearings are properly lubricated according to the manufacturer's guidelines.
  • ✔ Calibration: Periodically check the calibration of your CNC and laser machines to maintain precision.
