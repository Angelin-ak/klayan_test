Laser Technology: Revolutionizing Jewellery Marking

In the modern jewellery market, branding and hallmarking are more important than ever. Laser technology has emerged as the most efficient and precise tool for these tasks, replacing traditional mechanical engraving and stamping.

Benefits of Fiber Lasers

Fiber laser marking machines offer several advantages over CO2 or traditional methods. They provide a high-contrast marking that is permanent and does not affect the integrity of the metal.

  • ✔ Permanent Marks: The engraving will not fade or wear off over time.
  • ✔ Intricate Designs: Lasers can engrave complex logos and tiny hallmarks that are impossible to do by hand.
  • ✔ Speed: High-speed marking reduces turnaround time for large orders.
